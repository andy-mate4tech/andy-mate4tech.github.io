Before you fly

Winter in Tasmania packs differently.

July temps run 3–12°C in Hobart, well below freezing at Ben Lomond and Cradle Mountain. Pack for wind on the east coast and icy summit roads at Mount Wellington if you squeeze it in.

Outer layers
  • Waterproof winter jacket
  • Insulated puffer
  • Fleece mid-layer
Accessories
  • Beanies ×2 each
  • Waterproof gloves
  • Scarves, warm socks
Footwear
  • Waterproof walking shoes
  • Casual shoes
  • UGGs/slippers for the hotel
Ski day
  • Snow/ski pants — essential
  • Goggles + waterproof gloves
  • Thermal base layers
Highlands
  • Extra warm layer for Cradle Mountain
  • Sturdy shoes for Dove Lake boardwalks
The car
  • Phone charger + aux cable
  • Snacks, water bottles
  • Motion sickness tablets — winding roads
