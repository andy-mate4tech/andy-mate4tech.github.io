🧭 About adding Strahan: Strahan sits on the wild west coast — a genuine 4–4.5 hour drive from everywhere else on this loop, so there's no way to fold it into the existing 8 days without cutting something big. This version adds one extra night in Strahan and stretches the trip to 9 days / 8 nights, which means moving your return flight from Sun 19 Jul to Mon 20 Jul (contact Jetstar to shift JQ756) and booking one more night's accommodation. One knock-on effect: Salamanca Market only runs on Saturdays, and Saturday is now your Strahan → Hobart transit day — so you'll get Salamanca Place itself on the Sunday, just without the market stalls. If you'd rather keep the original flight dates, the easiest fix is a scenic flight/seaplane day trip to Strahan instead of driving — ask at Cradle Mountain Helicopters.

Before you fly

Winter in Tasmania packs differently.

July temps run 3–12°C in Hobart, well below freezing at Ben Lomond and Cradle Mountain, and Strahan's west coast weather can turn wild and wet with little warning off the Southern Ocean.

Outer layers
  • Waterproof winter jacket
  • Insulated puffer
  • Fleece mid-layer
Accessories
  • Beanies ×2 each
  • Waterproof gloves
  • Scarves, warm socks
