## 🚙 Car Rental Recommendation

For a family of 4 with luggage plus ski gear, a **7-seater SUV** is strongly recommended for comfort and safety on mountain roads.

| Option | Vehicle Type | Est. Price (7 days) | Suitable For |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|--------------|
| **🥇 Recommended** | **Toyota Kluger / Kia Sportage SUV (7-seater)** | **~$580–680** | Best value, plenty of space for luggage + skis |
| Premium Option | Toyota Land Cruiser Prado (4WD) | ~$780–950 | Ultimate safety for alpine roads |
| Budget Option | Mitsubishi Outlander (7-seater) | ~$420–500 | Cheaper but less powerful for mountain roads |

### ⚠️ Important Rental Notes

- ❄️ **Snow chains are legally required** when driving to Ben Lomond National Park in July. Most rental companies don't provide these — you'll need to **purchase or hire snow chains** (~$40–60) from outdoor stores in Launceston or Hobart before your ski day.
- 🛡️ **Add full insurance coverage** — Tasmanian roads can have wildlife (wallabies, wombats) active at dawn/dusk.
- ⛽ Fuel is ~$1.80–2.00/L in Tasmania. Total trip fuel budget: **~$220–260**.
- 🚫 **Never drive after dark** if possible — wildlife collisions are common.

| Item | Price |

**⚠️ ESSENTIAL SKI PREPARATION:**
